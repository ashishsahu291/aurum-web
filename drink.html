Special Selections From Our Cellar

White

2005 William Fevré Valmur Grand Cru, Chablis $260

Beautiful wine with diverse character. A rich core of oatmeal and hazelnuts is countered by intense lemon, green apple, and minerals.

2008 Joseph Phelps Ovation, Sonoma Coast $120

Almonds, butter, apples and citrus, finishes with a bit of acidity and a touch of smokiness.

Red

2004 Kosta Browne Amber Ridge Vineyard, Russian River Valley $255

Bursting with blueberry fruit and meaty notes. Graphite, minerals and oak cut across the midpalate finishes dry. (this one needs at least a half an hour to open up)

2007 Ridge Dusi Ranch Late Harvest, Paso Robles $205

Glycerin and raisined fruit on the nose. With concentrated stone fruits on the palate with well integrated tannins and nice medium finish. (Needs some time to breathe)

1999 Sherwin Family Vineyards, Spring Mountain $240

Fruit and floral nose. Soft and round, with an excellent balance of restrained wild berry fruit, zesty acidity, cassis, tobacco, chocolate and leather notes. Finishes long and slightly sweet, with fully resolved tannins.

Cabernet Sauvignon, Silver Oak, Napa Valley, 1996 $295

The 1996 Napa Valley Cabernet Sauvignon has a medium to dark ruby color. It has a complex, expressive nose of cassis, violets and sandalwood. This wine has a rich attack, a mouth-coating mid-palate and great return of fruit on the long, medium tannic finish
