Aurum Philosophy

Aurum prides itself on recreating diverse regional delicacies, both popular and nostalgic, and reinterpreting Indian traditions, to create an experience that toasts the old and celebrates the new. The kitchen presents the classic yet unexplored charm of India’s varied regions, expertly matched with familiar flavors and local California produce.

Our Strength

Our passionate team is a combination of experienced professionals with years of experience in food service. Their work combines food science, technology, and the culinary arts to imagine the future of food. They draw on current trends and culinary insights from across the globe to create customized solutions for all of our guests. Using intensive flavor research and renowned Indian hospitality, our team of forward-thinking professionals aims to guide the next phase of foodservice. Individually, they've earned accolades across the industry, but together they're crafting the future of flavor!
